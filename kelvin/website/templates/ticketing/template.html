üöá Accessing the University

The university is a 5-minute walk from the nearest Subway station: Hillhead. This provides links to Central Station, Queen Street Station, and Buchanan Bus Station. A Park and Ride service is also available at Kelvinbridge, Bridge Street, and Shields Road Subway stations.

There is limited free parking available on the UofG campus during the weekend. For more information, please see the UofG website.

For those with additional accessibility needs, parking can be found on the south side of the building. This is accessed from the eastern end of University Avenue, next to Kelvin Way.

üó∫ Accessing Bute Hall

Enter the Main Building of the university (with the big tower) and climb the stairs to reach either the East or West Quadrangle. Head to the Cloisters, between the two Quadrangles, where members of the Ensemble will direct you to the Bute Hall.

For those who are unable to use stairs, there is an accessible entrance on the south side of the building, beside the flagpole. From there, a lift is available to access the Bute Hall.

Videos containing directions to Bute Hall will be shared on our social media channels in the lead up to the concert.
