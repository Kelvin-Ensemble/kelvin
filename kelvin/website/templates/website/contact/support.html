The Kelvin Ensemble was founded as a chamber orchestra in 1991 by a group of enterprising students. It as since grown into a full-scale symphony orchestra. By becoming a Friend of the Kelvin Ensemble you can help us to continue and expand our much-loved institution, which provides fantastic opportunities for young people at Glasgow University.

We offer several different support packages, starting from just £20 per season (September-September).

Friends £20-49

• Two discounted tickets to all concerts

• Priority seating

• Named acknowledgement in both concert programmes

Maestro Friends £50-99

• Four discounted tickets to all concerts

• Priority seating

• Named acknowledgement in concert programmes

Patron £100+

• Four free tickets

• A further two discounted tickets

• Priority seating

• Named acknowledgement in concert programmes

Corporate Sponsorship

• Logo and link on our website

• Full page advertisement in both concert programmes

• Logos on all posters and other promotional material

• 15 minute talk or video at our annual ball

• Free tickets for up to four people

• Priority seating
