About the audition

The audition will consist of:

  1. one prepared piece of your own choice
  2. an excerpt that can be found below

Your chosen piece should be approximately 3-5 minutes long - the panel may stop you after this time.

Weekend auditions will take place in the University Music Department, 14 University Gardens (Apple Maps, Google Maps) - just along from the Queen Margaret Union on the 28th and the 29th of September.
Tuesday evening auditions will be in the Concert Hall, Main Building (next to Hunter Halls) on the 1st of October.

Bookings

To book an audition please select a time below and complete the booking process. Can percussionists please book auditions on the Tuesday evening. If you are having any difficulties, please contact webmaster@kelvin-ensemble.co.uk. Please can players arrive ten minutes before the time they have booked so they can warm up.
