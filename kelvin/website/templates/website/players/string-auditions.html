About the audition

The audition will consist of:

  1. one prepared piece of your own choice
  2. an excerpt that can be found below

Your chosen piece should be approximately 3-5 minutes long - the panel may stop you after this time. Violinists wanting to audition for leader will also be asked to play a piece of sight reading.

Weekend auditions will take place in the University Music Department, 14 University Gardens (Apple Maps, Google Maps) - just along from the Queen Margaret Union on the 5th and 6th of October.
Tuesday evening auditions will be in the Concert Hall, Main Building - A12 on the campus map (next to Hunter Halls) on the 1st of October.

Excerpts

Please find the excerpts below:

  • Violin excerpt
  • Viola excerpt
  • Cello excerpt
  • Double bass excerpt
Players will be assessed on rhythmical and musical accuracy over tempo.

Bookings

To book an audition please select a time below, and complete the booking process. If you are having any difficulties, please contact webmaster@kelvin-ensemble.co.uk. Please can players arrive ten minutes before the time they have booked so they can warm up.
