About the Audition

We are pleased to announce that we will hold face to face auditions this year!

In the auditions, we’d like to hear the prepared orchestral excerpts for your instrument (and doubling instrument if applicable) and a short piece of your choice that you feel shows your ability in its best light. Don't worry if you have to cut the piece short.

Your auditions will be assessed by the audition panel.

Please check below in the applicable category to see the excerpts, sign up, and book your audition slots.

The closing date for auditions is the 27th September.

BWP (Brass, Wind & Percussion)


The excerpts and audition information can be found here.

Percussion auditions will be held on Tuesday 1st October at the University of Glasgow Concert Hall, from 6pm-9pm.
Brass & wind auditions will be held on Saturday 5th October & Sunday 6th October at 14 University Gardens, from 9am-5pm.
