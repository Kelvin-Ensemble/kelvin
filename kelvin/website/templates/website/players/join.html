Video auditons now open!

We’re really excited to see that you’re interested in applying for Kelvin! At Kelvin we audition to help make the orchestra as satisfying and challenging as possible while keeping things fair, we can’t fit 14 trumpets in, for example. The orchestra is auditioned new each year with everyone auditioning – even the committee!

Due to the restrictions, it is unlikely that there will be any playing in 1st semester. All other GU music societies are having the same issues. We will, however, be ready to begin rehearsals whenever possible and in the meantime work on online projects and socials. :) We won’t meet until it is safe and legal.

About the audition

What we’re looking for is a video where you introduce yourself (so we know who’s audition it is) and then show off your playing. We’d like to hear the excerpt for your instrument (and doubling instrument if applicable) and around 3 minutes of a piece of your choice that you feel shows your ability in its best light. Feel free to cut your piece short, we don’t need to hear the whole thing. Once you’ve submitted your video please fill out this form to complete your audition.

Your audition will only be watched by the audition panel and will be deleted once auditions are over.

The closing date for auditions has been extended to 11th October.
